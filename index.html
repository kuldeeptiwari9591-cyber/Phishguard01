How PhishGuard Works

PhishGuard closes the gap between basic blockers and professional forensics. We analyze the "DNA" of a website—its age, owner, certificate, and behavioral patterns—to spot fakes that look real.

The Problem

Hackers create thousands of "burner domains" daily. They look like Netflix, PayPal, or your bank but are less than 24 hours old with hidden ownership.

Our Solution

We check global registries in real-time. If a "Bank" site was created yesterday by an anonymous owner, we flag it instantly with forensic evidence.

Community Power

User reports are aggregated to build a community-driven threat intelligence network, making detection faster and more accurate.
